Visualizing Sea Level Rise Across Puerto Rico (2015 – 2025)

This project explores how sea level has changed over the past decade at four coastal NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration) tide stations in Puerto Rico: San Juan, Parguera, Vieques, and Mayagüez. Using NOAA Tides and Currents, the sea level is measured in meters relative to a station-specific reference point, and the plots below compare trends and anomalies over time.

The map above shows the location of the four tide stations selected for this project, one in each cardinal direction across Puerto Rico.
